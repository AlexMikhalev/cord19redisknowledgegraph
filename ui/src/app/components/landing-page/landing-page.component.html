Explore medical literature graphically, leveraging industry knowledge and avoiding confirmation bias

Information exploration should be spacial preferably in VR

When exploring topics on science or engineering, you look at the diversity of the opinion, not the variety of the same cluster of words, same thought. You'd want to avoid confirmation bias and find articles relevant to the same concept, not necessary the ones which have similar words.
